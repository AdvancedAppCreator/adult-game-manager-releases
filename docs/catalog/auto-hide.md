# Auto-hide non-games

Auto-hide helps reduce clutter from installed apps that are clearly not games.

## Where to find it

Catalog or main-menu cleanup tools when available.

## What it does

Adult Game Manager can hide known non-game apps or rows you marked as not in catalog. Hidden rows are not deleted from your device.

## Undo

Use filters or row actions to show hidden/not-in-catalog entries again, then clear the hidden or not-in-catalog state.
